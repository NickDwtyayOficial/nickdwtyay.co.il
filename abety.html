Abety

Abety is a new language model created by Nicássio Guimarães, founder of Nick Dwtyay Ltd.

About Abety

Abety is a large language model, also known as a conversational AI or chatbot trained to be informative and comprehensive. It is trained on a massive amount of text data, and can communicate and generate human-like text in response to a wide range of prompts and questions. For example, Abety can provide summaries of factual topics or create stories.

The name "Abety" is an affectionate name that Nicássio Guimarães gave to start a conversation with ChatGPT. The meaning of Abety is "Alegria em Bits", which means "Joy in Bits" in Portuguese.

Nicássio Guimarães explained that ChatGPT is an important tool for our society and that establishing a right answer to your questions is a key part of its mission.

Features

  • Can provide summaries of factual topics
  • Can create stories
  • Can answer your questions in an informative way
  • Can generate different creative text formats of text content, like poems, code, scripts, musical pieces, email, letters, etc.

How to use Abety

To use Abety, simply type your question or prompt into the text box and click "Ask Abety." Abety will then generate a response.

You can also use Abety to create stories. To do this, type in the beginning of your story and then click "Create Story." Abety will then continue the story for you.
